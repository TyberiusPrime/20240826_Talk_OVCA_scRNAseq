Ovarian carcinoma biology

  • 4 subgrops of epithelial OC
    • Serous
    • Mucinous
    • Endometrioid
    • Clear cell
  • High grade serous OC (HGSOC) - most common
  • HGSOC sensitive to platin based chemotherapy
  • p53 mutated (>95%)
  • Copy number driven
  • serous tubal intraepithelial carcinoma
